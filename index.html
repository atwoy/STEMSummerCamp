The Goonies

The Goonies is about a group of kids who want to go on a final adventure before their homes are foreclosed. The main characters, Michael "Mikey" Walsh and Brandon "Brand" Walsh, and Mikey's friends, Clark "Mouth" Deveraux, Richard "Data" Wang, and Lawrence "Chunk" Cohen, find a treasure map in the Walshes' attic and they want to find the treasure. The 4 friends get Brand tied to an armchair, while they go try and get the treasure. Unfortunately, Brand, along with 2 girls, Andy and Stef, catch Mikey and his friends at a hideout. Brand tells the boys that they should go home, but the boys want to find the treasure.
